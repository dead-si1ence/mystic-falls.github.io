The Characters

Niklaus Mikaelson

Backstory: Born as Niklaus Mikaelson in the late 10th century, Klaus is the son of a wealthy Viking landowner and Esther, a powerful witch. However, Klaus's true father was a werewolf, a secret that led to deep familial strife. After Esther's infidelity was discovered, Klaus's stepfather, Mikael, hunted down and killed his biological father and his entire family, triggering Klaus's dormant werewolf gene. This revelation led Esther to create the spell that turned her children into the first vampires in history—The Originals. Klaus, however, became a unique hybrid of vampire and werewolf, with a deep-seated desire to form his own family to replace the one that betrayed him. Over the centuries, Klaus became known for his ruthlessness, temper, and ambition, yet he was haunted by his need for love and approval, particularly from his siblings.

Character Introduction: Klaus Mikaelson is the central figure of The Originals. As the Original Hybrid, Klaus possesses the combined strength of a vampire and a werewolf, making him one of the most powerful beings in existence. He is charismatic, strategic, and volatile, with a deep-seated fear of abandonment that drives many of his actions. Despite his ruthless nature, Klaus shows moments of vulnerability, particularly in his interactions with his daughter, Hope, and his complex relationships with his siblings.

Alias: The Hybrid

Elijah Mikaelson

Backstory: Elijah, the second-born Mikaelson sibling, is the epitome of the noble vampire. Born in the late 10th century, Elijah was deeply affected by his family's transformation into vampires. He swore to uphold family unity at all costs, often serving as the moral compass and diplomat among his siblings. Over the centuries, Elijah became known for his adherence to honor and his penchant for formal attire, earning him the nickname "the Noble Stag." Despite his loyalty to Klaus, Elijah is constantly torn between his sense of duty and his own desires, especially when it comes to love and the possibility of a life free from his brother's shadow.

Character Introduction: Elijah Mikaelson is Klaus’s older brother and the family’s mediator. Calm, composed, and honorable, Elijah often takes on the role of peacemaker, both within his family and in their dealings with others. He believes in the concept of "Always and Forever," a vow he made to his siblings centuries ago, but this often puts him at odds with his personal happiness. Elijah's deep love for his family, especially Klaus, often leads him to make difficult sacrifices.

Alias: The Noble One

Hayley Marshal

Backstory: Hayley was born Andrea Labonair, a member of a powerful werewolf clan in New Orleans. Her parents were killed when she was a baby, and she was placed in foster care, unaware of her true lineage. Hayley spent most of her life on the run, trying to uncover the truth about her past. Eventually, she met Tyler Lockwood, a hybrid, and through him, she was introduced to the supernatural world. In a bid to break the sire bond that linked hybrids to Klaus, Hayley had a one-night stand with Klaus, which resulted in an unplanned pregnancy. This pregnancy, carrying the first hybrid child in history, became the catalyst for her deep involvement with the Mikaelson family.

Character Introduction: Hayley Marshall is a fierce and independent werewolf who becomes deeply entwined with the Mikaelsons after becoming pregnant with Klaus's child. Initially, she is focused on finding out more about her werewolf heritage, but the birth of her daughter, Hope, changes her priorities. Hayley becomes fiercely protective of her new family, determined to create a safe and stable life for her daughter in a world fraught with danger.

Alias: The Crescent Wolf

Marcel Gerard

Backstory: Marcel Gerard was born in the early 19th century as the son of a slave. After his mother died, he was taken in by Klaus Mikaelson, who saved him from a cruel plantation owner. Klaus raised Marcel as a son, teaching him everything he knew about power and survival. Marcel grew up to become a charismatic leader, eventually taking control of New Orleans after the Mikaelsons fled the city. Under Marcel's rule, the city became a haven for vampires, but his reign was also marked by strict rules to maintain the peace between the various supernatural factions.

Character Introduction: Marcel Gerard is a charismatic and ambitious vampire who rose from humble beginnings to become the self-proclaimed king of New Orleans. He has a complicated relationship with Klaus, viewing him both as a father figure and a rival. Marcel is fiercely loyal to those he cares about but is also ruthless in his pursuit of power and order in the city. Despite his often brutal methods, Marcel’s ultimate goal is to create a city where all supernatural beings can coexist peacefully.

Alias: The King of New Orleans

Rebekah Mikaelson

Backstory: Rebekah Mikaelson, the youngest of the Mikaelson siblings, was turned into a vampire by her mother, Esther, in the 10th century. Rebekah has always longed for a normal life, desiring love, family, and stability. However, her loyalty to her brothers, especially Klaus, has kept her from achieving this dream. Throughout the centuries, Rebekah has fallen in love numerous times, but Klaus’s overprotectiveness and fear of abandonment have often sabotaged her relationships. Despite her resentment towards Klaus for ruining her chances at happiness, she remains deeply devoted to her family.

Character Introduction: Rebekah Mikaelson is the loving yet fiercely independent sister of Klaus and Elijah. She is beautiful, passionate, and determined to live life on her terms. Rebekah often finds herself torn between her desire for a normal human life and her unbreakable loyalty to her family. Her longing for love and a family of her own is a central part of her character, making her the most human of the Original siblings.

Alias: The Crescent Wolf

Davina Claire

Backstory: Davina Claire was a powerful young witch from New Orleans, born into a coven with a tragic history. As a teenager, Davina was chosen to be the Harvest Girl, a ritual meant to restore power to the witches of New Orleans. However, the ritual was interrupted, leaving Davina alive but with immense power that she struggled to control. Isolated and feared by many, Davina was eventually taken in by Marcel, who became a mentor and protector to her. Despite her youth, Davina's power and determination made her a formidable force in the supernatural community.

Character Introduction: Davina Claire is a young but incredibly powerful witch who quickly rises to prominence in New Orleans’ supernatural community. Initially manipulated by those around her, Davina grows into her power, becoming a strong and independent force. Her relationships with Marcel, who she sees as a father figure, and with the Mikaelsons, especially Kol, are central to her character development. Davina's struggle to balance her power with her desire for a normal life makes her one of the most compelling characters in the series.

Alias: The Harvest Witch

Camille O'Connell

Backstory: Camille "Cami" O'Connell was a human psychologist with a tragic family history. Her twin brother, Sean, was manipulated into committing a mass murder-suicide by supernatural forces, an event that deeply scarred Cami and drove her to seek answers about the supernatural world. Despite being a human, Cami quickly became involved with the Mikaelsons, particularly Klaus, who was drawn to her intelligence, compassion, and resilience. Cami’s psychological insight often provided Klaus with a perspective he had never considered before, making her an invaluable ally.

Character Introduction: Camille O'Connell is a human psychologist who becomes entangled with the supernatural elements of New Orleans, particularly through her relationship with Klaus Mikaelson. Intelligent, compassionate, and determined to understand the truth about her brother’s death, Cami is unafraid to confront the darkness within others, including Klaus. Her moral compass and deep empathy make her a grounding force in the often chaotic world of the Mikaelsons.

Alias: The Human Bartender

Freya Mikaelson

Backstory: Freya Mikaelson is the eldest child of Esther and Mikael, born in the late 10th century. Believed to have died as a child, Freya was actually taken by her aunt Dahlia as part of a dark bargain Esther made to have children. Freya spent centuries as Dahlia's prisoner, enduring unimaginable suffering and being forced into magical servitude. She eventually escaped and reunited with her family in the 21st century. Despite her tragic past, Freya is a powerful witch and fiercely loyal to her family, though she often struggles with the trauma of her long captivity.

Character Introduction: Freya Mikaelson is the eldest Mikaelson sibling, a powerful and determined witch who endured centuries of torment under her aunt Dahlia’s control. After reuniting with her family, Freya becomes the Mikaelsons' most formidable magical ally, often using her powers to protect them from supernatural threats. Though she is still healing from her traumatic past, Freya is fiercely protective of her siblings and is determined to build a new life with them.

Alias: The First Witch

Kol Mikaelson

Backstory: Kol Mikaelson, the youngest brother, was also turned into a vampire by Esther in the 10th century. Known for his impulsive and reckless behavior, Kol often found himself at odds with his siblings, particularly Klaus. Over the centuries, Kol's lust for power and disregard for human life led to a tumultuous existence. Despite his dark nature, Kol has always felt overshadowed by his older brothers, particularly Elijah and Klaus. His yearning for recognition and respect drives much of his behavior. Kol’s relationship with Davina Claire marked a significant turning point in his character, as he began to explore the possibility of redemption and love.

Character Introduction: Kol Mikaelson is the most unpredictable and rebellious of the Mikaelson siblings. He is charming, mischievous, and often driven by his desire to prove himself. Kol’s dark impulses and tendency to act on whims have caused rifts between him and his family, but he is also deeply loyal when it comes to those he cares about. His love affair with Davina Claire adds layers to his character, revealing a softer, more vulnerable side.

Alias: The Trickster

Episodes

Episode 1: "Always and Forever"

Synopsis: The Mikaelson siblings return to New Orleans after a century away, only to find that the city has changed and new threats have emerged. Klaus, Elijah, and Rebekah must navigate the treacherous waters of supernatural politics to protect their family and reclaim their rightful place as the rulers of the French Quarter.

Episode 2: "House of the Rising Son"

Synopsis: Klaus's protege, Marcel, has taken control of New Orleans in the Mikaelsons' absence. When a powerful witch threatens the delicate balance of power in the city, Klaus and his siblings must decide whether to ally with Marcel or confront him head-on.

Episode 3: "Tangled Up in Blue"

Synopsis: Hayley, a werewolf with a mysterious past, arrives in New Orleans seeking answers about her family. Klaus, intrigued by her connection to his own bloodline, offers to help her in exchange for information about the witch who has been targeting his family.

Lore

The Mikaelson Family

The Mikaelson family is one of the oldest and most powerful vampire bloodlines in existence. Born in the late 10th century, the Mikaelsons were originally human, but a series of events led to their transformation into the first vampires in history. The family consists of five siblings: Klaus, Elijah, Rebekah, Kol, and Finn. Each sibling has unique abilities and characteristics that have shaped their interactions with each other and the supernatural world at large.

The French Quarter

The French Quarter of New Orleans is a supernatural hotspot, home to vampires, witches, and werewolves. The Quarter has a long and storied history of supernatural activity, dating back to the city's founding. Over the centuries, various factions have vied for control of the Quarter, leading to a delicate balance of power that is constantly threatened by internal and external forces.

The Harvest Ritual

The Harvest Ritual is a powerful magical ceremony performed by the witches of New Orleans. The ritual is meant to restore power to the witch community and maintain the balance of nature. However, the Harvest Ritual has a dark history, marked by betrayal and tragedy. The consequences of the interrupted ritual have reverberated through the supernatural community, leading to conflict and unrest among the various factions.
